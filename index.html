How To Buy

Buy and send BNB to MetaMask

Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet in form of a browser extension) or TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that you will have to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network-list. (Click here for a step-by-step tutorial)

Buy and send BNB to MetaMask

Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Kraken, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a "0x"

Head on to PancakeSwap and swap for SAFEMIND

Click here to head on over to PancakeSwap. Set the slippage tolerance to 8% (sometimes it may be a bit more, depending on how much demand there is)

View SAFEMIND and HODL

Swap BNB for SAFEMIND. Now you need to add SAFEMIND to your MetaMask (Tutorial here) or Trust Wallet to view your SAFEMIND. Lastly HODL!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Safe MInd?
We are on a journey to fund projects, charities and organisations that will help with mental health issues around the world. This will be done through regular fundraisers, donations and charity events which will be all supported through our strong community and tokenomics. Being with SafeMind, allows you to help those who are experiencing mental problems that become a life pain, and also helps you earn by just holding.
Will my rewards always be paid hourly ?
Payouts to holders may not occur every hour if trading volume is low. When this is the case, the amount of rewarded BNB each holder will receive does not decrease. However, will accrue over a longer period of time until it is suitable for a payout to trigger (usually a few hours).
